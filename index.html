Explaining how Cannabis can impact all 5 dimensions of health and wellbeing.


Cannabis can affect all dimension of health and well being, for physical health, cannabis can affect appearance, like bloodshot eyes, but also less visible physical health, like having a dry mouth or impaired coordination

for mental health, cannabis has quite an addictive power, where somebody could become dose dependent, and experience anxiety or depresssion, due to thinking that everything depends on this as well as worries that they won't survive without it

as for social health, cannabis would get you the fast track into some not so good groups of people, those who supply drugs, those also addicted and those who are shady, this would also affect the way you act to others as the people who surround you would act as if this was normal

for emotional health, cannabis is very good at making you feel quite good, as if everything is okay and peaceful, and creates the illusion that what cannabis does to you is all good and not bad whatsoever

and finally, spiritual health, cannabis can stimulate spiritual connection, mainly through the euphoria felt when taking a dose of it, although it doesn't really do anything to help with spiritual health at all.
